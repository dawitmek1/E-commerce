About SNEAKHUB

Founded in 2023, SNEAKHUB began as a small sneaker store in Addis Ababa with a big dream: to bring the latest and greatest footwear to sneaker enthusiasts across Ethiopia. What started as a passion project has grown into one of the premier destinations for premium sneakers in the region.

At SNEAKHUB, we believe that sneakers are more than just shoes - they're a statement of personal style and a reflection of individuality. That's why we carefully curate our collection to offer only the highest quality, most stylish footwear from the world's top brands.

Our Mission

To provide sneaker enthusiasts with access to premium footwear while delivering exceptional customer service and building a community around sneaker culture.

Our Vision

To become the leading sneaker destination in Africa, known for our curated selection, authentic products, and commitment to sneaker culture.
